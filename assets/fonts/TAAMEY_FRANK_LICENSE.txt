Taamey Frank CLM (TaameyFrankCLM-Medium.ttf, TaameyFrankCLM-Bold.ttf)

A digitization of the classic Frank-Rühl Hebrew typeface with full nikud and
te'amim support, by the Culmus project (Maxim Iorsh et al.).
